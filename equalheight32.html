Online enrolment for new students

If you are:

  • starting a new course
  • transferring to a completely new course (after having withdrawn from another course)
  • progressing from undergraduate to postgraduate level
  • progressing from postgraduate taught to postgraduate research level
  • progressing from a partner institution
  • an exchange student.

Online enrolment for returning students

If you are:

  • continuing the next stage of a course you have already started
  • transferring to a completely new course (without having withdrawn from another course)
  • progressing from a year-zero foundation course
