BEd / PGCE study trip to The Gambia

You'll participate in a number of activities during a week-long visit to the Gambia including visiting the capital; the main market town of Serrekunda; a Primary school and Nursery in Mandinari. You will have the opportunity to teach; eat a traditional meal with a local family; visit a women’s cooperative; visit a rural compound and meet the family; visit two animal sanctuaries; spend a morning with a musician; and have time to explore student led activities.
