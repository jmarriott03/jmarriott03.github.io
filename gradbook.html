Leave Application

Status: Approved

Please use this form to request the approval of Annual Leave.

All PGR students have an allowance of 8 weeks leave per year, which includes public bank holidays. This form should be completed BEFORE the annual leave has taken place and before you leave the University.

The Compliance Team must be kept informed when you are on leave, so we can assist you in maintaining your visa status and so we can explain your situation to UKVI officers if you are stopped at the UK border. You must inform us if the period of absence stated on this form becomes longer. Extenuating circumstance procedures should still be followed as per usual procedures.

It is your responsibility to ensure this information reaches your Director of Studies and the UKVI Compliance Team, by completing this form and your Director of Studies approving it. If we have no record of why you are not here your enrolment and visa may be cancelled. The UKVI Compliance Team will assess the information provided, and will inform the student and Faculty of any implications for the student's visa and ability to study in the UK.
