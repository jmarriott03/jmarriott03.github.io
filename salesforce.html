Continuing professional development

We have the largest range of continuing professional development (CPD) degree and master courses in the South West, all of them with fantastic student support and many offering flexible study patterns and a variety of bursaries and scholarships, so the University of Plymouth can be the place for you! Our open evenings are a great opportunity to meet with lecturers, go along to talks in subject areas that interest you, and chat with current students. Search our courses for full information on deadlines and entry requirements and apply as soon as possible to make your plans a reality.
