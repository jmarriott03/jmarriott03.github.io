Case study

Relatable

  • What does the content mean to the end user?

Journey

  • What/who is the case study about?
  • What was the goal/needs?
  • How was this met?

Include facts/figures

  • What was the return on investment?
  • Be clear - X & Y over a period of 6 months led to an increase of Z

Formatting

  • Body text minimum 16px = 1em
  • Sans serif fonts are more accessible
  • Width is no more than 80 characters
  • Text is not justified
  • Text is aligned left
  • Line spacing (leading) is at least space-and-a-half within paragraphs
  • Paragraph spacing is at least 1.5 times larger than the line spacing
  • High contrast between text and background
  • Is video with transcript more suitable than text & images?
