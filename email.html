Dear Jacques Rippin,

As part of your application, you may be asked to provide financial documents to show you meet, or will be able to meet, the Student route financial requirement prior to applying for your visa. This may include but is not limited to:

  • Recent bank statements from the account that you intend to use to demonstrate you meet the financial requirement during the visa process
  • If you are using funds from your parents account, you will need to provide evidence to prove your relationship to the owner of the account
  • Scholarship Loan letter if applicable demonstrating the amount of funds that will be covered by your scholarship or loan

For more information on what is required, please review the Evidence of Finances section of our website: https://www.plymouth.ac.uk/international/apply-for-a-student-visa

If you have any questions about the evidence required, please contact credibilitysupport@plymouth.ac.uk
