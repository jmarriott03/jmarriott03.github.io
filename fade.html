Undergraduate open day

Student ambassadors and members of staff welcome you to the campus and help you get the most out of your open day.

Programme

To help you plan your day in advance we will send you a copy of the programme one week before the day.

A full printed version of the programme will also be available for you to collect from registration when you arrive at the Open Day.

Arrival

The undergraduate open day runs from 9.00am until 4.30pm.

On arrival to the campus, please collect your goody bag and register your attendance with a member of the registration team in the Roland Levinsky Building.

The registration team will talk you through the programme for the day and answer any questions you may have.

We strongly advise that you check the times of the talks and subject sessions that you are interested in, as some sessions are only run once.
