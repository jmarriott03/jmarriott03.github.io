University IT Roadmap

Becoming a Digital University
2018/19 - 2023/24

Strategic themes

One team. One University. One IT plan.

Welcome to University of Plymouth’s IT Road Map. Developed with key stakeholders. A rolling plan driven by our academic mission.

  • Apply the technologies, processes, practices and culture of the digital age.
  • Create and operate joined-up digital services for learning, teaching and research.
  • Work as one team to meet increased student expectations in a digital world.

Together, we will become a thriving digital University.

IT that works

Our preferred solutions will be highly intuitive, resilient and secure by design.

  • Operate rolling refresh programmes to ensure the University’s IT offer remains current.
  • Scale our IT security efforts to match cyber-threat levels.
  • Control access to systems and data, and we will fulfil our IT legal compliance obligations.
  • Enable a student-run IT shop to sell equipment and provide repair services.
  • No-fuss IT that ‘works’.

Increase digital capabilities

  • Equip our people to be digitally capable and innovative.
  • Take an institution-wide approach to rationalising and joining-up our systems and data to support our processes.
  • Create a single University data model and establish data owners.
  • Use data analysis and visualisation tools to create actionable insights and inform decision making.
  • Work together to transform the University into a smooth running digital organisation for our students.

Seamless end-to-end digital experience

  • Offer our students the choice to experience our University entirely digitally.
  • Use automation, artificial intelligence, social media and other enabling technologies to reach new levels of effectiveness and efficiency.
  • Build our next generation digital learning and assessement environment, and use digital to underpin our research lifecycle.
  • Transform our piecemeal digital experience into one which is seamless end-to-end.
