Dr Rafi Goodman is Lecturer in Marine Molecular Biology and Director of the Molecular Institute at the University of Plymouth.

Rafi is a world-leading marine scientist and is at the forefront of pioneering research into the causes and effects of molecular biology. She founded and heads the University’s International Molecular Biology Research Unit, which has charted the global distribution of molecules from Arctic sea ice to the deep seas.

In 2020, the University was awarded the Queen's Anniversary Prize for the pioneering research of Rafi and her colleagues on molecular biology and its impact on the environment and changing behaviour.
