Find an Education Agent

Plymouth University has employed a network of authorised education agents around the world to assist you with program and application queries

Our authorised education agents can provide you with:

  • Accurate information about Plymouth University and our programs
  • Answer your questions and advise you about our programs and entry requirements
  • Assist with your application and verifying your supporting documentation
  • Assistance with your student visa application
  • Assist with travel, accommodation and pre-departure arrangements

Please note that some agents may charge a fee for these services.

Select your country or region from the drop down menu below and then select the "Search" button. Please select the agent name to see detailed information.

If there are no registered agents in your country or region, please contact us for advice on your study options and assistance with your application.
