What is Plymouth Connect?

Welcome to Plymouth Connect, a professional network for students and alumni of Plymouth University. Here you can connect with Plymouth University alumni to share contact details and career information, network online and stay in touch.

eMentoring
eMentoring is designed to help alumni and current students connect so that alumni can advise and guide students and graduates on their professional journey. This could be anything from a few emails or checking a CV/application, to having a telephone conversation or even offering work experience. The idea is that alumni can give as much or as little of their time and expertise as they can spare to help.

Re-connecting
Plymouth Connect allows you to enter your current contact details, details of your time at Plymouth, and information about your current job. You can then decide how much of this information you are happy to share with others on the Directory. Log-in details will only be given to alumni, students and staff of Plymouth University.

Keeping in touch
The Directory is a great starting point if you are trying to get back in touch with an old University friend, or to find Plymouth alumni in your area or in your field of work. It can also be used simply to update the contact details the University holds for you

Alumni map
This map helps you see where in the country, or indeed the world, other Plymouth alumni are based. This could be useful if you are visiting an area for a work placement, on business, or completely relocating and keen to connect with others who share your experiences of Plymouth!
