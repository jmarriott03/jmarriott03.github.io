Specialisms

For maximum flexibility and optionality, students can study 3 modules from any other programme within the School (2 at L5 and 1 at L6). If they elect to undertake 3 modules from the same discipline, they can achieve an exit award on their certificate.

The specialism modules can either be taken from Sociology, or from any programme in the school.
