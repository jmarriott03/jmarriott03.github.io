Do

  • Choose a photo that looks like you
  • Make sure your face takes up at least 60% of the frame
  • Choose the right expression
  • Wear what you’d wear to work
  • Choose a background that isn’t distracting
  • Keep photo current
  • Smile with teeth
  • Squinch
  • Accentuate your jawline
  • Make eye contact
  • Head to shoulders or head to waist in frame

Avoid

  • Sunglasses
  • Face only close-ups
  • Full body shots
  • Dark colours
  • High colour saturation
