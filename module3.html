Aims

To show how the global importance of microbes can be explained by an understanding of their genes, genomes, metabolic diversity, ecology, and evolution.

Learning outcomes

At the end of the module you should be able to:

  • Explain how microbes control gene expression and adapt to their environment (LO1).
  • Discuss the metabolic diversity and ecology of microbes, and their roles in biogeochemical cycles (LO2).
  • Explain modern concepts in microbial evolution and systematics (LO3).
  • Understand and apply modern laboratory techniques for the study of microbes and communicate the results of investigations effectively (LO4).
  • Formulate and discuss an individually selected microbiological research topic (LO5).
