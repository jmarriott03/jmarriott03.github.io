The University Executive Group (UEG) is the primary executive decision-making body, working with the Academic Board and the Board of Governors. It has a formal responsibility for providing effective leadership and direction, and developing the University Strategy for discussion by Academic Board and approval by the Board of Governors. It is also responsible for leading implementation of the Strategy once approved, including the resolution of potentially conflicting priorities, and monitoring and reporting to the Board of Governors on delivery against Key Performance Indicators and on institutional sustainability. UEG meets fortnightly and publishes a UEG summary of discussions (log in required) and their outcomes after each meeting. UEG operates a number of Executive Advisory Groups and communications fora.
